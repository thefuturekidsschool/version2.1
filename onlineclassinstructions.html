THE FUTURE KIDS SCHOOL, RAJAHMUNDRY (AP104)

The Future Kids School,Rajahmundry takes immense pleasure in announcing it's progression into the next phase of its Online Mentoring Program by providing to it's students the facility of live classes. We request you to download the ZOOM app for this purpose. The ZOOM app can be downloaded in laptop, desktop, mobile phone or a tab. Please make use of headphones / earphones to experience better sound quality.

Kindly note a few points in this regard:

1. The Online Live Classes for our ISC students(Grades XI & XII) are being conducted successfully. The Online Live Classes for students into Grades VIII to X will start from 13.04.2020.

2. The Schedule (time/subject) will be shared with you shortly. SMS will also be sent sharing with you the details of the class.

3. To maintain the efficiency of the class, we suggest the students to attend the online session in an isolated space of their homes, to avoid any disturbances.

4. The online classes will be locked after five minutes into the scheduled time, so we suggest the students to be on time for these online classes.

5. Please do have a pen and a notebook with you to take notes.

6. While you are in the online class, kindly mute your microphones and disable your videos. You may unmute to ask your doubts or when the teacher addresses you.

7. We request the parents to monitor their wards during these online sessions.
