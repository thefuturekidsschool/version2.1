As the fight against Covid 19 is putting us under a strict lockdown, the resilience of human spirit finds ways to keep going. 
To use this lockdown efficiently,and to continue its mentoring process,

The Future Kids School,
has started an
ONLINE MENTORING PROGRAM (OMP),
which goes live on
03.04.2020 9:00 AM.

The parents and students are requested to make diligent use of the ONLINE MENTORING PROGRAM.

A few points to be noted in this regard:
    • The OMP encompasses the students going into Grades I to XII.
    • The students going into Grades I to III are given an assignment schedule to be followed from 06.04.2020 to 25.04.2020.
    • The students going into Grade IV and Grade V are given an assignment schedule to be followed from 03.04.2020 to 25.04.2020.
    • For the students going into Grade VI and above, online classes are enabled along with the corresponding assignments.
    • There is a STUDY SCHEDULE given at the top of every page for the corresponding grade. Accordingly, the

      student can navigate to the appropriate subject and lesson and watch the online classes and complete their Assignments on time.

    • All the students must submit their assignments on the reopening day.
    • For the students of 2019-20 Grade X, an appropriate study schedule has been prepared keeping in mind the board exams that are yet to be done.
    • For the students of 2019-20 Grade XII, an appropriate study schedule, to help them in their Competitive exams, has been put up.
    • The online classes for Grade VI and above will be renewed according to the study schedule on a weekly basis.
    • We request the parents to monitor the students as they avail the OMP.
